曉曉曉

「曉曉曉曉曉，哈哈哈哈哈哈。」

方文工程的方克文說，只要是爬樓梯上唐樓的工程，他甚麼都曉。這個曉是擬聲詞，隨即他示範「曉曉」聲的大力呼吸：「行完樓梯索晒氣，曉吓曉吓，咪乜都曉咯！」然後哈哈大笑。

荃灣發展和方克文的生活轉化同步：政府發展這個新市鎮，他就在市鎮的工廠工作，又在這裏建立一個小工程店：到工廠修理門鉸、修理電線——一切都已成過去。但改變的，除了宏觀的社區變遷，也呈現在微觀的一個物件上。因為修好一個物件，很好玩！

這是一個遊戲：在沒有說明書，沒有指引之下，以經驗與技術完成的猜謎。有一樣東西能動腦筋絕對是快樂事，打機不也是這樣嗎？這些街坊生意，也一直就像猜謎，也做到人「曉吓曉吓」，然後哈哈哈哈。

Fong Man Engineering Company

"Hiu Hiu Hiu Hiu Hiu, hahahahahahaha." Fong hackman, a construction worker, said that he “Hiu”(Knows) everything when he climbing stairs and working on the old buildings.

The word "Hiu" is an echoic of the inhalation sound. Fong immediately demonstrated : After climbing the stairs and catching my breath, I “Hiu”!

Then he burst into laughter.

The development of Tsuen Wan and Fong’s life transformed simultaneously: as the government developed this new town, he worked in the town's factory and also established a small engineering shop here. Repairing hinges and fixing wires - everything has become a thing of the past. But what has changed, apart from the community changes, is also reflected in the object. Fixing an object is a joy!

This is a game: a puzzle solved without instructions or guidance, only by your skills and experience. It's always enjoyable to engage your brain in something. Isn't playing video games the same?

These neighborhood businesses have always been like game, making people catch the breath, "Hiu Hiu", and laugh.

方文工程公司
1973年至現今
新界 荃灣 香車街街市 22號檔 地下
受訪者：方克文先生
業務性質：小型工程

方文工程是香港一家小型工程公司由方克文先生創立,他在荃灣發展新市鎮期間,開始在當地做一些小型維修工程。方克文先生是一位擅長於維修各種機械和電子設備的技工。隨著荃灣的發展,方文工程在當地的工廠和社區提供維修服務,還建立了自己的小型工程店。方克文先生致力於為客戶提供高質量、貼心的維修服務,體現了香港小型企業的勤勞與創意精神。

Fong Man Engineering Company
1973 to present
Shop 22, Heung Che Street Market and Cooked Food Centre, 8 Tso Kung St, Tsuen Wan
Interviewee: Mr. Fong Hak Ma
Business:Minor Works

Fong Man Engineering is a small engineering company in Hong Kong, founded by Mr. Fong Kok-man. He started doing small repair work in the local area during the development of the Tsuen Wan new town. Mr. Fong Kok-man is a technician skilled in repairing various mechanical and electronic equipment. As Tsuen Wan developed, Fong Man Engineering provided repair services to local factories and communities, and also established its own small engineering shop. Mr. Fong Kok-man is committed to providing high-quality and thoughtful repair services, embodying the diligence and creativity of Hong Kong's small enterprises.
