About the Team
關於我們

Space And

我們，黃詠珊（Pat Wingshan Wong）和陳家智（Kachi Chan），掌舵著 Space And。 Space And 是一個始於香港的藝術家團體，其誕生源於記錄和參與不斷變化的社會文化景觀的願景。我們的關注點橫跨有形、數位和潛在空間，以城市和技術領域的快速發展為標誌。我們致力於批判性地分析和重新想像這些空間及其影響，尤其是它們與社會經濟敘事、監控和等級制度的交集。

We, Pat Wingshan Wong and Kachi Chan, helm Space And, an artist collective started in Hong Kong, birthed from a vision to document and engage with our transforming socio-cultural landscapes. Our focus spans tangible, digital, and latent spaces marked by rapid developments within urban and techno spheres. We strive to critically analyse and reimagine these spaces and their effects, particularly as they intersect with socio-economic narratives, surveillance, and hierarchy.
