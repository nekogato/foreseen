縫補寶
2018年至現今
新蒲崗 六合街 23號 萬昌中心 1樓 106室
受訪者：劉先穎女士
業務性質：復康人士服裝

縫補寶康健服裝有限公司是一家香港本地的社會企業,專注於設計、生產和銷售針對長者及其照顧者的康健用品。它聘請本地婦女和退休人士,為他們提供車縫康健服裝和週邊商品的工作機會。通過提供就業機會和提升照護意識,縫補寶康健服裝有限公司成為了一個兼顧社會公益和商業發展的初創企業。

Adaptive Clothing
2018 to present
Room 106, Max Trade Centre, 23 Luk Hop St, San Po Kong
Interviewee: Ms. Lau Sin Wing
Business:Rehabilitation clothing

Adaptive Clothing Limited is a social enterprise in Hong Kong, focusing on the design, production and sale of healthcare products for the elderly and their caregivers. It employs local women and retirees, providing them with job opportunities to sew healthcare clothing and related products. By providing employment opportunities and raising care awareness, Adaptive Clothing Limited has become a startup that balances social welfare and business development.
